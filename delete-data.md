# Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of any data that **Popup Pulverizer** stores about you. There are two ways to do this depending on whether you still have the app installed.

---

## Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

If you still have Popup Pulverizer installed:

1. Open the app.
2. Open the **Config** menu (gear icon).
3. Scroll to the **Privacy & Data** section.
4. Tap **"DELETE ALL DATA"**.
5. Confirm.

This immediately:

- Wipes all local data on your device (preferences, progression, identity, premium status).
- Sends a request to our server to delete your leaderboard entries, nickname registration, and recovery code mapping.

You will receive on-device confirmation when the deletion completes.

---

## Option 2 — Email request (if you uninstalled the app)

If you have already uninstalled the app and still want to delete server-side data tied to your old install:

**Email:** [support@hkenterprises.io](mailto:support@hkenterprises.io)

**Subject:** `Data deletion request — Popup Pulverizer`

**Include in the body:** any of the following that you remember (more info = faster lookup):

- Your **leaderboard nickname**
- Your **recovery code** (format: `WORD-WORD-NNNN`, e.g. `EMERALD-WOLF-7294`) if you saved one
- Approximate first-played date

We will:

1. Locate any server-side records tied to your former device identity.
2. Delete leaderboard scores, nickname registration, recovery code mapping, and any verified purchase records.
3. Reply with confirmation within **30 days** (typically much sooner).

---

## What gets deleted

| Data | Deleted by in-app button | Deleted by email request |
|---|---|---|
| Local game data (preferences, progression, premium status) | ✅ Immediately | ⚠️ Already gone if you uninstalled |
| Leaderboard scores | ✅ Server call | ✅ Manually |
| Nickname registration | ✅ Server call | ✅ Manually |
| Recovery code mapping | ✅ Server call | ✅ Manually |
| Anonymous crash reports (Sentry) | Auto-purged after 90 days | Auto-purged after 90 days |
| Anonymous gameplay telemetry | Anonymous — not tied to identity | Anonymous — not tied to identity |

---

## What we do NOT store

We do not store and never have access to:

- Your real name
- Your email address (other than what you provide if you email us)
- Your phone number
- Your physical address
- Your Apple ID or Google account email
- Your payment card details

The only personally identifiable information you give us in normal use is your chosen leaderboard nickname (which you can already change in-app).
