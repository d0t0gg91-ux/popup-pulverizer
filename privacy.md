# POPUP PULVERIZER PRIVACY POLICY

**Effective: April 25, 2026**

---

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Gameplay Data:** Nickname (user-chosen, not a real name), scores, level, and ads-closed counts for leaderboard functionality.
- **Device Identity:** An anonymous UUID generated on your device. Not linked to any hardware identifier, advertising ID, or personal information.
- **Preferences:** Sound volume, avatar selection, and privacy choices — stored locally on your device.
- **Technical Data:** Our ad partners (including Google AdMob) may collect device type, IP address, and interaction data to serve ads and prevent fraud.
- **Gameplay Telemetry:** Anonymous event counts (ad taps, shop views, premium-button taps, session length, crash-free status) to help us balance and improve the Game. No nickname, device identity, or payment data is attached. You can disable this anytime in the Config menu.
- **Purchase Receipts:** When you make an in-app purchase, Apple or Google sends a receipt (including transaction ID and product ID) to our server so we can verify the purchase with the respective platform. Your credit-card number, billing address, and Apple/Google account email are NEVER shared with us — they stay with the App Store / Play Store.
- **Operator's Club Age Verification:** The first time you enter the Operator's Club mini-game, we ask for your date of birth on a single screen. We use this once to compute whether you are 18 or older, then DISCARD the date entirely. Only a single boolean ("18+ verified" or "denied") and the verification timestamp are saved on your device — never your date of birth, and never transmitted anywhere. Lawful basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — the verification is necessary to operate an age-restricted feature, and a date-of-birth check is the least-intrusive method that satisfies regulator guidance (CNIL, ICO, Irish DPC).

## 2. How Data Is Stored

Gameplay data and preferences are stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores and identity data are transmitted to our backend (Supabase) using TLS encryption.

## 3. Third-Party Partners

- **Google AdMob** — serves ads. May use device identifiers to personalize ads or measure performance. Opt out of personalized ads in the Config menu.
- **Supabase** — stores your anonymous device identity, nickname, and leaderboard scores over a TLS-encrypted connection.
- **Sentry** — collects anonymized crash reports and error diagnostics (device model, OS version, stack traces) to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is attached.

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

This Game is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us for removal.

EU member states set the age of digital consent between 13 and 16 (Germany, Netherlands, Ireland, Poland, Croatia, Hungary, Romania, Slovakia, Luxembourg = 16; France = 15; Austria, Italy, Spain, Bulgaria, Cyprus, Lithuania = 14; remaining states = 13). Where the local floor exceeds 13, parental authorization is required for users below that floor. Separately, the Operator's Club mini-game is hard-gated to users aged 18 and over regardless of the local digital-consent floor — see Section 1 (Operator's Club Age Verification) for the data-minimisation flow we use.

## 5. Your Rights

- Opt out of personalized ads in the Config menu
- Opt out of anonymous gameplay telemetry in the Config menu
- Delete all stored data via "DELETE ALL DATA" in the Config menu. This clears your local data AND removes your leaderboard entries from our servers.
- EU/EEA users have additional rights under GDPR including access, rectification, and erasure

## 6. Data Retention

Local data is retained until you delete it via Settings or uninstall the Game. Leaderboard data is stored on our servers.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date at the top will reflect any changes.

## 8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: **support@hkenterprises.io**
